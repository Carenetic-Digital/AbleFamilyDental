# Able Family Dental — Doctor Bios

---

## Dr. Dan Anderson

Dr. Anderson has been passionate about dentistry since growing up in Magrath, Alberta, where he first envisioned his future in the field. Today, he is proud to live out that dream by caring for families throughout the Lethbridge and Magrath communities. After graduating from high school in Magrath, he went on to earn his dental degree from the University of Alberta.

For over 20 years, Dr. Anderson has been dedicated to providing compassionate, high-quality dental care to his patients. He values building lasting relationships with the families he serves and takes pride in supporting the health and well-being of his community.

Outside of his professional life, he enjoys spending time outdoors and participating in a variety of water sports. He and his wife, Tara, are the proud parents of four children: Devon, Megan, Braden, and Ethan.

## Dr. Mark Still

Born and raised on a ranch in Mountain View, Alberta, Dr. Mark Still developed a strong work ethic and appreciation for community early in life. While he valued his upbringing on the farm, he discovered at a young age that his true passion was dentistry.

He attended high school in Cardston before pursuing post-secondary studies at the University of Alberta in Edmonton, where he earned his Bachelor of Science degree. He went on to complete his dental education in 2011.

Since joining Able Family Dental in 2013, Dr. Still has built meaningful relationships with both his patients and his team. He is known for his compassionate approach and dedication to helping individuals achieve and maintain healthy, confident smiles.

Outside of the office, Dr. Still enjoys an active family life with his wife and their four children. Together, they spend much of their time camping and participating in sports and various activities. His passion for dentistry is matched by his genuine care for the people he serves, making him a valued member of his community.

## Dr. Tyler Snow

Dr. Tyler Snow and his lovely wife Morgan both grew up in southern Alberta and are happy to call it home. They have four beautiful children and Dr. Snow enjoys spending as much of his free time as he can being with them.

Dr. Snow began his education at the University of Lethbridge and went on to the University of Alberta to complete a Bachelor of Medical Science and a Doctorate of Dental Surgery. He enjoys the variety of services that general dentistry involves. Dr. Snow is certified to provide several forms of sedation, including nitrous oxide (laughing gas), oral sedation, and moderate conscious IV sedation. This allows him to provide treatment for patients who struggle with dental anxiety or require more invasive surgical treatment such as wisdom tooth removal. Dr. Snow is also Neuromodulator Level 2 certified, allowing him to provide Botox for both upper face wrinkles and teeth grinding.

When he is not doing dentistry or playing with his kids, Dr. Snow enjoys watching football, making barbecue, and working on his old trucks.
