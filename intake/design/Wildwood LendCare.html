Patient Financing

Flexible payment options with LendCare

We offer patient financing through LendCare, a Canadian healthcare financing provider. LendCare allows you to spread the cost of dental treatment over time with flexible payment plans, making it easier to move forward with recommended care without having to pay everything upfront.

Apply online in minutes
or ask our team in clinic. Apply with LendCare
How does LendCare work?
Applying for financing is simple. You can complete an application online or ask our team for assistance during your visit. If approved, you'll be able to select a payment plan that fits your budget and pay for treatment over time rather than all at once.
What treatments can be financed?
Financing can be applied to a wide range of dental treatments, from restorative and cosmetic care to major procedures such as crowns, implants, orthodontics, and oral surgery. If you're unsure whether your treatment qualifies, our team is happy to confirm during your visit.
Is approval guaranteed?
Approval is not guaranteed. LendCare reviews each application individually, and financing is subject to credit approval. Many patients do qualify, and our team can help you start an application to find out which options are available to you.
Can I make monthly payments?
Yes. LendCare offers flexible monthly payment plans so you can spread the cost of treatment over time instead of paying everything upfront. Once you're approved, you'll be able to choose a plan that fits your budget.
What are the payment terms?
Payment terms vary depending on the amount financed and your individual qualifications. Term lengths, rates, and monthly amounts are presented to you before you accept any offer, so you can decide what works best for you.
Who can I contact if I have questions?
Our team is always happy to help. Call us at 306.374.7272 or ask at your next appointment, and we'll walk you through the financing options and answer any questions about applying through LendCare.

We're here to help.

Have questions about financing options or whether your treatment may qualify? Our team is happy to walk you through the process and help you find the best solution for your needs.
