What is the CDCP?

The Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP) is a federal government program designed to make dental care more affordable for eligible Canadian residents who do not have dental insurance. The program helps cover a portion of the cost of many dental services.

They can learn more here

Do You Qualify for the CDCP?

No Dental Insurance

You do not have access to private dental insurance.

Family Income Under $90,000

Your adjusted family net income is under $90,000 per year.

Canadian Resident

You are a Canadian resident for tax purposes.

Filed Taxes

You filed your tax return in the previous year.

What Services May Be Covered?

The CDCP may help cover a portion of the cost for many dental services such as:

Dental exams and X-rays Root canals Cleanings and preventive care Periodontal treatment Fillings and restorative care Some oral surgery services

Some services may require pre-authorization before they are covered. Depending on your household income, the program may cover between 40% and 100% of eligible treatment costs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the CDCP cover all dental treatment?
Not always. The CDCP helps cover a portion of the cost for many common dental services, but it may not cover the full cost of treatment, and some procedures fall outside the plan. Our team will review what is covered for your specific care before treatment begins.
Will I have any out-of-pocket costs?
Possibly. Depending on your adjusted family net income, the program may cover between 40% and 100% of eligible costs, and some services may exceed CDCP fee limits. We will always explain any expected out-of-pocket amount before your appointment.
Do I need to renew my coverage?
Yes. CDCP coverage must be renewed each year after you file your annual income tax return. Keeping your taxes up to date ensures your eligibility continues without interruption.
Can Wildwood Dental Clinic help me understand my coverage?
Absolutely. Our team is happy to walk you through what the CDCP covers, what to expect, and any costs associated with your treatment so there are no surprises.
Can I use my CDCP benefits at Wildwood Dental Clinic?
Yes. Wildwood Dental Clinic is pleased to support patients who qualify for the CDCP. Let us know you are covered when you book, and we will help coordinate your care.
Do some services require pre-authorization?
Some procedures do require pre-approval before they are covered under the CDCP. Our team will identify these in advance and handle the necessary steps with you.
How do I apply for the CDCP?
Patients apply directly through the Government of Canada, online through your My Service Canada Account (MSCA). You can find the application and full eligibility details on the Government of Canada website.
What if I am unsure whether I qualify?
Review the eligibility requirements on the Government of Canada website, or give us a call. We are glad to point you in the right direction so you can find out whether the CDCP is available to you.
