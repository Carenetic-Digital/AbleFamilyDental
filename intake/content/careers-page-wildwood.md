**WORKING AT WILDWOOD DENTAL CLINIC**

Wildwood Dental Clinic has been part of the Saskatoon community for over
35 years. Our team of dentists, hygienists, assistants, and
administrative staff work together in a collaborative environment
focused on providing consistent, quality care to patients and families
across the city.

**ROLES AT OUR CLINIC**

Dentists\
General dentists who provide comprehensive care across all aspects of
clinical dentistry, from preventive and restorative work to cosmetic and
surgical procedures.

Dental Hygienists\
Registered dental hygienists who perform professional cleanings,
periodontal assessments, and patient education, a central part of our
preventive care model.

Dental Assistants\
Clinical support staff who work chairside with our dentists, prepare
treatment rooms, manage sterilization protocols, and help ensure each
patient visit runs smoothly.

Administrative & Reception\
Front-desk and administrative team members who manage patient
scheduling, insurance billing, communications, and the day-to-day
operations of the clinic.
