WHAT IS THE CDCP?
=================

The Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP) is a federal government program
designed to make dental care more affordable for eligible Canadian
residents who do not have dental insurance.

The program helps cover a portion of the cost of many dental services.

### Learn More About the CDCP

Learn more directly from the Government of Canada.

LINK:
https://www.canada.ca/en/services/benefits/dental/dental-care-plan.html

DO YOU QUALIFY FOR THE CDCP?
============================

You may qualify if you meet all of the following criteria:

### No Dental Insurance

You do not have access to private dental insurance.

### Family Income Under \$90,000

Your adjusted family net income is under \$90,000 per year.

### Canadian Resident

You are a Canadian resident for tax purposes.

### Filed Taxes

You filed your tax return in the previous year.

### Check Eligibility Details

Learn more about eligibility requirements directly from the Government

WHAT SERVICES MAY BE COVERED?
=============================

The CDCP may help cover a portion of the cost for many dental services
such as:

• Dental exams and X-rays

• Cleanings and preventive care

• Fillings and restorative care

• Root canals

• Periodontal treatment

• Some oral surgery services

Some services may require pre-authorization before they are covered.

Depending on your household income, the program may cover between 40%
and 100% of eligible treatment costs.

IMPORTANT NOTES ABOUT CDCP
==========================

• The CDCP may not cover the full cost of treatment

• Some procedures require pre-approval

• Patients must renew their CDCP coverage each year after filing their
annual income tax return

• Additional charges may apply depending on the treatment and coverage
limits

Our team will help explain your benefits and discuss any costs before
your appointment.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
==========================

### Does the CDCP cover all dental treatment?

No. The CDCP may cover only a portion of eligible dental treatment
costs. Coverage amounts depend on household income and the specific
service being provided.

### Will I have any out-of-pocket costs?

Possibly. Some treatments may not be fully covered, and additional
charges may apply depending on your treatment needs and CDCP coverage
limits.

### Do I need to renew my coverage?

Yes. Patients must renew their CDCP coverage each year after filing
their annual income tax return.

### Can Wildwood Dental Clinic help me understand my coverage?

Yes. Our team can help explain your CDCP benefits and discuss any costs
before treatment is provided.

### Can I use my CDCP benefits at Wildwood Dental Clinic?

Wildwood Dental Clinic welcomes patients who qualify for the Canadian
Dental Care Plan.

### Do some services require pre-authorization?

Yes. Certain treatments may require pre-authorization before coverage is
approved under the CDCP.

### How do I apply for the CDCP?

Applications are completed directly through the Government of Canada
using your My Service Canada Account (MSCA).

### What if I am unsure whether I qualify?

Eligibility requirements are determined by the Government of Canada.
Please review the official eligibility requirements for the most current
information.
