Frequently Asked Questions
==========================

New Patients
------------

### Are you accepting new patients?

Yes. Wildwood Dental Clinic welcomes new patients and families from
Saskatoon and surrounding communities.

### What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first appointment may include a review of your medical and dental
history, a comprehensive examination, any necessary X-rays, and a
discussion about your oral health goals and treatment options.

### What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your insurance information, a list of medications you are
currently taking, and any relevant dental records if available.

### How often should I visit the dentist?

Most patients benefit from regular examinations and hygiene
appointments, although the recommended frequency may vary based on
individual oral health needs.

### Can I transfer from another dental office?

Yes. If you are changing dental providers, our team can help coordinate
the transfer of records when appropriate.

### Can you request records from my previous dentist?

Yes. With your authorization, we can assist in obtaining relevant
records from your previous dental office.

Children's & Family Dentistry
-----------------------------

### Do you see children?

Yes. Wildwood Dental Clinic welcomes children of all ages and provides
dental care for the entire family.

### When should my child have their first dental visit?

The Canadian Dental Association recommends a child's first dental visit
within six months of the first tooth erupting or by their first
birthday.

### Can my children and I book appointments on the same day?

Whenever possible, we are happy to accommodate family scheduling
requests.

### What if my child is nervous about visiting the dentist?

Our team works to create a positive and comfortable experience for
children and will take the time to help them feel at ease during their
visit.

### Do you provide preventive care for children?

Yes. We offer examinations, hygiene appointments, fluoride treatments,
and other preventive services tailored to children's oral health needs.

Insurance, CDCP & Financing
---------------------------

### Do you accept the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)?

Yes. Wildwood Dental Clinic welcomes eligible patients covered under the
Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP).

### Does CDCP cover all dental treatment costs?

Coverage varies depending on eligibility, treatment needs, and the
services covered under the program. Some services may require a
co-payment or may not be fully covered.

### Will I know my costs before treatment begins?

Yes. We strive to provide clear information regarding treatment
recommendations, fees, insurance coverage, and any potential
out-of-pocket expenses.

### Do you offer direct billing to insurance?

We can assist patients with submitting insurance claims and help explain
available coverage whenever possible.

### Do you offer financing options?

Yes. Financing may be available through LendCare for eligible patients
seeking assistance with treatment costs.

### What is LendCare?

LendCare is a third-party financing provider that offers payment plans
designed to help patients manage the cost of dental treatment over time.

### Can I finance treatment if I don't have dental insurance?

Many patients use financing options to help manage treatment costs
regardless of whether they have dental insurance coverage.

### Can I use insurance and financing together?

In many cases, patients use both insurance benefits and financing
options to help manage treatment costs.

Appointments & Scheduling
-------------------------

### How do I book an appointment?

Appointments can be booked by contacting our office directly by phone or
through the appointment request options available on our website.

### Can I book online?

Patients may request appointments online through our website.

### What if I need to reschedule my appointment?

If you need to reschedule, please contact our office as soon as possible

### How early should I arrive for my appointment?

New patients are encouraged to arrive a few minutes early to complete
any necessary paperwork and provide insurance information.

Emergency Dental Care
---------------------

### What is considered a dental emergency?

Dental emergencies may include severe tooth pain, swelling, dental
infections, broken teeth, knocked-out teeth, or injuries affecting the
mouth or jaw.

### Can I get a same-day emergency appointment?

We make every effort to accommodate dental emergencies as quickly as
possible. Please contact our office directly for assistance.

### What should I do if I knock out a tooth?

Contact our office immediately. Prompt treatment may improve the chances
of saving the tooth.

### What should I do if I have a severe toothache?

Contact our office as soon as possible. A severe toothache may indicate
an underlying issue that requires professional evaluation.

Dental Treatment
----------------

### What if I haven't been to the dentist in several years?

Many patients return to dental care after an extended absence. Our team
is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and judgment-free
experience.

### Are dental procedures painful?

Modern dental techniques and local anesthetics are designed to help
patients remain comfortable throughout treatment.

### I feel nervous about dental treatment. Can you help?

Yes. Dental anxiety is common. We will discuss your concerns and review
options that may help you feel more comfortable during your visit.

### How do I know if I need treatment?

A comprehensive examination allows your dentist to assess your oral
health and discuss any recommended treatment options, including the
benefits, risks, and alternatives.

### Do you provide dental implants?

Yes. We offer dental implant consultations and treatment options for
eligible patients.

### Do you provide root canal treatment?

Yes. Root canal treatment may be recommended when the inner portion of a
tooth becomes infected or damaged.

### Do you offer teeth whitening?

Yes. We provide professional teeth whitening options and can discuss
whether treatment is appropriate for your needs.

### Do you offer treatment for sleep apnea and snoring?

Yes. Oral appliance therapy may be an option for some patients
experiencing snoring or obstructive sleep apnea.

Seniors & CDCP Patients
-----------------------

### Do you welcome senior patients?

Yes. We provide dental care for patients of all ages, including seniors.

### Can you help me understand my CDCP coverage?

Our team can help explain how your coverage may apply to recommended
treatment and discuss any potential out-of-pocket expenses.

### What if I am on a fixed income?

We believe patients should understand all available treatment options
and associated costs. Our team can discuss insurance coverage, CDCP
eligibility, and financing options when appropriate.

Professional Standards & Patient Safety
---------------------------------------

### Are your dentists licensed in Saskatchewan?

Yes. All dentists at Wildwood Dental Clinic hold a Doctor of Dental
Medicine (DMD) degree and are licensed to practise dentistry in
Saskatchewan.

### What infection prevention procedures do you follow?

Wildwood Dental Clinic follows current infection prevention and control
protocols designed to support patient and staff safety.

### What is the Commitment to Clean™ program?

As part of the Canadian Dental Services Corporation network, our clinic
participates in the Commitment to Clean™ program, which supports
standards for cleanliness, instrument sterilization, and infection
prevention.

### How do you support patient safety?

Patient safety is a priority at Wildwood Dental Clinic. We follow
established clinical protocols, sterilization procedures, and infection
prevention practices designed to support safe and effective dental care.
